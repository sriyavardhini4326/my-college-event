About the Event

Sankranthi Celebrations at ISTS Women's Engineering College The vibrant festival of Sankranthi, marking the harvest season, was celebrated with great enthusiasm and traditional fervor at ISTS Women’s Engineering College. The event brought together students and faculty to experience the rich cultural heritage of India. Highlights of the Event Traditional Rangoli Competition: The day began with a colorful Rangoli competition, where students showcased their creativity through intricate designs depicting the essence of Sankranthi. The vibrant patterns added a festive charm to the college premises. Cultural Performances: Students performed traditional dances like Kolatam and showcased folk songs that resonated with the spirit of the festival. Their graceful movements and soulful music captivated the audience. Bhogi Mantalu: A symbolic Bhogi Mantalu (bonfire) was organized to signify the start of the celebrations. Participants gathered around the bonfire, embracing the warmth and joy of the occasion. Ethnic Attire Contest: An ethnic wear contest was held, where students donned traditional sarees and dresses. Their stunning attires reflected the diversity and elegance of Indian culture. Games and Activities: Various traditional games such as Kabaddi, Kite Flying, and Pot Breaking (Matka) added an element of fun and excitement. The lively participation from students created a cheerful atmosphere. Special Feast: A delectable feast featuring Pongal, Garelu, and other Sankranthi delicacies was arranged, allowing everyone to savor the flavors of the festival.
